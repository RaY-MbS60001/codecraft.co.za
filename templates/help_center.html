Getting Started

Creating an account is simple! You have two options:

  • Google Sign-In: Click "Sign in with Google" on the homepage and authorize access using your Google account.
  • Email Registration: Click "Register" and fill in your details including name, email, and password.

After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link to verify your account and start applying for learnerships!

A learnership is a structured learning program that combines theoretical education with practical workplace experience. It's a great way to:

  • Gain industry-recognized qualifications
  • Get hands-on work experience
  • Earn a stipend while learning
  • Improve your employment prospects

Learnerships typically last 12-24 months and are registered with the relevant SETA (Sector Education and Training Authority).

Eligibility requirements vary by learnership, but general requirements include:

  • South African citizen or permanent resident
  • Age between 18-35 years (varies by program)
  • Minimum of Grade 12 / Matric certificate
  • Currently unemployed or seeking to change careers

Each learnership listing will specify its exact requirements. Make sure to read them carefully before applying.

Applications

To apply for a learnership:

  • Log in to your CodeCraft Co account
  • Browse available learnerships on the dashboard
  • Click on a learnership to view details and requirements
  • Click "Apply Now" and complete the application form
  • Upload your CV and any required documents
  • Submit your application

You'll receive a confirmation email once your application is submitted successfully.

Common documents required for applications include:

  • Updated CV/Resume (PDF format preferred)
  • Certified copy of ID document
  • Certified copy of Matric certificate
  • Proof of residence (not older than 3 months)
  • Any additional certificates or qualifications

Some learnerships may require additional documents. Always check the specific requirements before applying.

You can track your applications through your dashboard:

  • Log in to your account
  • Go to "My Applications" section
  • View the status of each application (Pending, Under Review, Shortlisted, Accepted, or Rejected)

You'll also receive email notifications when your application status changes.

Yes, you can withdraw your application at any time before it's processed:

  • Go to "My Applications" in your dashboard
  • Find the application you want to withdraw
  • Click "Withdraw Application"
  • Confirm your decision

Note: Once withdrawn, you may need to wait before reapplying to the same learnership.

Account Settings

To update your profile:

  • Log in to your account
  • Click on your profile icon in the top right
  • Select "Profile Settings"
  • Update your information as needed
  • Click "Save Changes"

Keep your profile updated to improve your chances of being selected for learnerships!

To reset your password:

  • Go to the login page
  • Click "Forgot Password?"
  • Enter your registered email address
  • Check your email for the reset link
  • Click the link and create a new password

If you signed up with Google, you'll need to reset your password through Google's account settings.

If you wish to delete your account:

  • Go to "Profile Settings"
  • Scroll to "Account Management"
  • Click "Delete Account"
  • Confirm your decision

Warning: This action is permanent. All your data, applications, and history will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing loading issues, try these steps:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Try accessing from a different device

If the problem persists, please contact our support team.

Document upload issues are usually caused by:

  • File size: Maximum file size is 5MB per document
  • File format: Only PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, and PNG files are accepted
  • File name: Avoid special characters in file names
  • Connection: Ensure you have a stable internet connection

Try compressing your files or converting them to PDF format before uploading.

If you're not receiving our emails:

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Add noreply@codecraftco.com to your contacts
  • Verify your email address is correct in your profile
  • Check your email notification settings in your account
  • Whitelist our domain in your email provider

Still not receiving emails? Contact us at support@codecraftco.com
